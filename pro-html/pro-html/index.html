Why Eco Eco-Friendly

Eco friendly toys are better for the environment in some way. They might be recyclable or made with materials that break down over time. Or they might be made from natural or sustainably sourced materials. Some environmentally friendly toys may also be made using non toxic paints and materials. A double bonus of being safer for our little ones and kinder to the environment.
